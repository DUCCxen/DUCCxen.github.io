Adobong puso ng Saging
Description
Adobong Puso ng Saging is a Filipino dish made from banana blossoms (puso ng saging) cooked in a flavorful mix of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices. It has a slightly tangy and savory taste, often enjoyed with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana blossom (puso ng saging), cleaned and sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: ½ tsp sugar for balance

Allergens

Soy
Contains soy from soy sauce.

Steps

  1. Prepare the banana blossom by peeling the tough outer layers, slicing, and soaking in water with salt to reduce bitterness.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add the banana blossom and stir for a few minutes.
  4. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, and water, then let it simmer.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, adding sugar if preferred.
  6. Cook until tender, then serve with rice.
