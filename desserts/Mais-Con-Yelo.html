Mais Con Yelo
Description
Mais Con Yelo is a popular Filipino dessert made with layers of sweet corn kernels, shaved ice, and evaporated milk, often topped with sugar and sometimes a scoop of ice cream. Derived from the Spanish "Ma√≠z con hielo" (corn with ice), this refreshing treat is perfect for hot days. Some variations include condensed milk for added sweetness or crushed cornflakes for extra texture. Served in a tall glass or bowl, it combines creamy, icy, and chewy elements, making it a favorite, especially in the summer.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sweet corn kernels (canned or fresh)
  • 2 cups shaved ice
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk (optional, for extra sweetness)
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 scoop vanilla or cheese ice cream (optional)
  • Crushed cornflakes or pinipig (optional, for added crunch)

Allergens

Dairy
Mais Con Yelo includes Evaporated Milk and Condenced Milk. People with dairy allergies or lactose intolerance should be cautious.

Steps

  1. If using fresh corn, remove the kernels. If using canned corn, drain.
  2. Put a generous amount of corn in a glass.
  3. Fill the glass with shaved ice. Press it slightly and add more.
  4. Pour in the milk over the shaved ice.
  5. Sprinkle the sugar over the mixture.
  6. For extra flavor of your Mais Con Yelo, top it with a scoop of ice cream and pinipig.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
