Ginataang Bilo-Bilo
Description
Ginataang Bilo-Bilo is a warm and comforting dessert made with glutinous rice balls, coconut milk, and sweet ingredients like saba bananas, sweet potatoes, and jackfruit. The name “Ginataang Bilo-Bilo” translates to “glutinous rice balls in coconut milk.” It is a traditional Filipino dessert often served during cold weather or as a merienda (snack). Ginataang Bilo-Bilo is similar to other coconut-based desserts in Southeast Asia but is distinct for its chewy rice balls and hearty ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Water
  • Coconut milk
  • Sugar
  • Saba bananas, sweet potatoes, jackfruit, and tapioca pearls

Allergens

Dairy
While it's primarily made with coconut milk, some variations might include dairy or dairy-based ingredients, which can cause issues for those with dairy allergies.

Steps

  1. Mix glutinous rice flour with water to form small balls.
  2. Boil coconut milk and sugar. Add rice balls, fruits, and root crops.
  3. Cook until everything is tender. Serve warm.
