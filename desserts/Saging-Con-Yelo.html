Saging Con Yelo
Description
Saging Con Yelo is a Filipino dessert made with saba bananas, shaved ice, sweet syrup, and condensed milk. It's a cool and satisfying treat often enjoyed during hot days or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 4-6 saba bananas (or plantains, peeled and sliced)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 cups shaved ice
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup evaporated milk (optional)
  • 1/4 cup crushed ice (for added texture, optional)
  • 1 tablespoon butter (optional, for extra richness)
  • Crushed peanuts or sago pearls (optional toppings)

Allergens

Dairy
Saging Con Yelo includes Evaporated Milk and Condenced Milk. People with dairy allergies or lactose intolerance should be cautious.

Steps

  1. Prepare the bananas: Peel and slice the saba bananas into rounds or diagonally, depending on your preference.
  2. Caramelize the bananas: In a pan over medium heat, combine the sliced bananas, brown sugar, and water. Stir gently and let it simmer for about 5-10 minutes until the bananas are soft and the syrup thickens. Optionally, add butter for richness and flavor.
  3. Assemble the dessert: In a glass or bowl, layer shaved ice at the bottom. Then, add the caramelized bananas with the syrup on top.
  4. Add the milk: Pour condensed milk and evaporated milk (optional) over the dessert for a creamy texture and extra sweetness.
  5. Garnish: For added texture and flavor, you can top your Saging Con Yelo with crushed peanuts or sago pearls.
  6. Serve: Serve immediately as a refreshing dessert, perfect for a hot day!
