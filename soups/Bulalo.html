Bulalo
Description
Bulalo is a comforting Filipino beef dish, famous for its rich and flavorful broth made from beef shank and bone marrow. It's typically served with vegetables like corn, cabbage, and green beans, making it a hearty and satisfying meal enjoyed especially during cooler weather or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. beef shank (with bone marrow)
  • 1 medium onion (quartered)
  • 1 whole garlic bulb (crushed)
  • 2 pieces corn on the cob (cut into 2-3 parts)
  • 1 large potato (peeled and sliced)
  • 1 bunch cabbage (chopped)
  • 1/2 lb. green beans (trimmed)
  • 1-2 pieces saba bananas (optional, peeled and sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
  • 5-6 cups water
  • 1 piece Knorr Beef Cube (optional, for added flavor)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Allergens

Beef
Beef is a common allergen, and individuals with a beef allergy may experience symptoms such as hives, digestive issues, or more severe reactions.

Steps

  1. Prepare the beef shank: In a large pot, add the beef shank along with the bone marrow. Cover with water (about 5-6 cups), and bring it to a boil over medium-high heat. Once the water is boiling, skim off any scum or impurities that float to the surface.
  2. Simmer the beef: Lower the heat to medium-low and let the beef shank simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the meat becomes tender and the marrow begins to soften.
  3. Add the vegetables: Once the beef is tender, add the corn, potatoes, onion, and garlic. Let it cook for another 15-20 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.
  4. Season the broth: Add the fish sauce, Knorr beef cube (if using), and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir and let it simmer for another 5 minutes to let the flavors meld together.
  5. Final touches: Add the green beans and saba bananas (if using). Continue to simmer for another 5-10 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked through but still crisp.
  6. Add the cabbage: Lastly, add the chopped cabbage and cook for about 2-3 minutes until the cabbage wilts and becomes tender.
