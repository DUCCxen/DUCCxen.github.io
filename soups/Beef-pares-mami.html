Beef Pares Mami
Description
Beef Pares Mami is a comforting Filipino noodle soup dish made from tender beef stew and flavorful broth, served with noodles. It is often enjoyed as a snack or hearty meal, especially during rainy days or for special occasions. The sweet and savory broth, along with the tender beef and noodles, makes it a favorite for many Filipinos.

Ingredients

    Beef Pares (Beef Stew):

    • 1 lb. beef brisket or shank (cut into cubes)
    • 1 piece Knorr Beef Cube
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
    • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
    • 1 piece onion (minced)
    • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
    • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
    • 2 pieces star anise
    • 1 piece bay leaf
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
    • 4 cups water
    • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (optional)

    Mami (Noodles and Soup):

    • 2 packs egg noodles (cooked)
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
    • 2 boiled eggs (optional)
    • Chopped spring onions (for garnish)

Allergens

Wheat (Gluten)
The noodles used in Beef Pares Mami are often made from wheat, which contains gluten. This can trigger an allergic reaction in people with a wheat allergy or celiac disease.

Steps

  1. Prepare the Beef Pares (Beef Stew): In a bowl, combine the beef cubes, soy sauce, brown sugar, and cornstarch. Toss to coat the beef and let it marinate for at least 15-30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Add the minced onion and garlic. Saut√© until fragrant and the onions are soft.
  3. Add the marinated beef to the pot. Let it cook for 5-7 minutes, allowing the beef to brown slightly.
  4. Add the star anise, bay leaf, black pepper, and water. Stir and bring to a boil.
  5. Add the Knorr Beef Cube and fish sauce (optional), then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cover and cook for 1.5 to 2 hours or until the beef is tender and the broth has developed a rich flavor.
  6. Prepare the Noodles: While the beef is simmering, cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Once done, drain and set aside.
  7. In a separate pan, heat 1 tablespoon of cooking oil over medium heat. Add the cooked noodles, soy sauce, and white pepper. Toss to combine and heat through for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Assemble the Beef Pares Mami: To serve, place a portion of the noodles in a bowl. Ladle the beef stew and broth over the noodles. Optionally, add a boiled egg on top for extra flavor and garnish with chopped spring onions.
  9. Enjoy!
