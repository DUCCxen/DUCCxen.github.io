Ginataang Mais
Description
Ginataang Mais is a comforting Filipino dessert or snack made from glutinous rice, coconut milk (gata), and sweet corn. This warm, creamy porridge has a rich coconut flavor with the natural sweetness of corn, making it a favorite merienda (afternoon snack) or breakfast treat. It’s similar to other ginataan (coconut-based) dishes but has a unique texture from the combination of sticky rice and tender corn kernels.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous rice (malagkit na bigas), rinsed
  • 4 cups coconut milk (gata)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup sweet corn kernels (fresh, canned, or frozen)
  • ½ cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Allergens

Dairy
Coconut is classified as a tree nut by the FDA, though some people are allergic to it. Those with coconut allergies may experience reactions similar to other nut allergies.

Steps

  1. Cook the rice: In a pot, combine glutinous rice, coconut milk, and water. Bring to a gentle boil over medium heat.
  2. Simmer: Stir occasionally to prevent sticking, then reduce the heat and simmer until the rice is soft and the mixture thickens (about 15–20 minutes).
  3. Add the corn and seasonings: Stir in the corn kernels, sugar, and salt. Mix well and continue cooking for another 5–10 minutes until the flavors meld.
  4. Add vanilla (optional): If using, add vanilla extract for extra aroma.
  5. Cool and serve: Remove from heat and let it cool slightly. Serve warm in bowls, or chill in the fridge if you prefer it cold.
